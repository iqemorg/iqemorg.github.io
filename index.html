Institute for Quantum Electronics in Medicine (IQEM)

About IQEM

The Institute for Quantum Electronics in Medicine (IQEM) is an open-access research organization dedicated to exploring quantum effects in biological systems to accelerate the development of non-invasive medical technologies through quantum electronics. IQEM is committed to promoting education in quantum biology, and ensuring that all research, discoveries, and technologies remain freely accessible in the public domain.

Quantum Electronics in Medicine

Quantum electronics refers to the study and application of electronic and photonic systems that leverage quantum mechanical principles. This includes the development of quantum-based devices such as lasers and LEDs, which use quantum effects—stimulated emission and electron-photon interactions—to generate and control light for non-invasive medical treatments and diagnostics.
