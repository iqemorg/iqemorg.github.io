Institute for Quantum Energy in Medicine (IQEM)

About IQEM

The Institute for Quantum Energy in Medicine (IQEM) is an open-access research organization dedicated to accelerating the development and adoption of light-based therapies. IQEM is committed to ensuring that all research, discoveries, and technologies are freely accessible and remain in the public domain.​

Quantum Energy in Medicine

Quantum electronics refers to the study and application of electronic and photonic systems that leverage quantum mechanical principles. This includes the development of quantum-based devices such as lasers and LEDs, which use quantum effects—stimulated emission and electron-photon interactions—to generate and control light for non-invasive medical treatments and diagnostics.
